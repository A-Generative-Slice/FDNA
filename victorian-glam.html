High Fashion Collection

Victorian Glam

An immersive journey through 19th-century royal silhouettes reimagined for the modern era. This portfolio showcases the technical precision, textile curiosity, and artistic vision of Najeema Afrin.

Royal Victorian Heritage

The foundation of this collection explores the architectural volume of the Victorian era. By synthesizing tiered silk layers with modern construction, this design achieves a balance of historical weight and contemporary movement.

Midnight Sparkle: Black Velvet

A study in the interplay between light and dark. This design features high-pigment black velvet contrasted against ethereal sparkle tulle, representing the dramatic 'Glam' aesthetic of the collection.

The Lavender Blossom

Romanticism brought to life through silver embroidery and delicate sheer layers. The lavender tones provide a soft counterpoint to the heavier velvet pieces, rounding out the emotional arc of the collection.

Maroon Velvet Majesty

Showcasing industrial construction standards. This ballgown utilizes heavy velvet and a technical bodice for structural integrity, designed to meet the rigorous demands of the global export market.

The Modern Mermaid

A fusion of Victorian luxury and 21st-century silhouettes. The diamond mesh pattern overlay creates a dynamic play of light, proving that heritage colors can be radically modern.
